Welcome to PPE Detection

Feist Finches, our team, has developed an AI system designed to assist construction workers throughout their workday. While considering various AI applications, we deliberately selected this particular project. The completed AI has the potential to provide valuable support to real construction workers. Its functionality relies on identifying specific attributes that are unique to construction workers, such as helmets, vests, and goggles.

Sadly, numerous construction workers lose their lives each year due to various causes, with falls being the leading cause of fatalities. Wearing personal protective equipment (PPE) is the most effective means of preventing such accidents. By leveraging our AI, we aim to save lives and enhance the safety of construction workers.
